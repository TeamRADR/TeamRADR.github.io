Our Research Focus

Broadly the research lab's current focus is two-fold namely EEG based Machine learning applications and Neuroimaging based Subgrouping and Brain connectivity.
    The Realtime P300 speller developed in our lab is illustrated in the video below:


Other than this, research into novel cognitive paradigms using brain computer interfaces and dynamic audio visual stimuli for educational neuroscience applications, are being conducted. These include diverse fields such as Neurocinematics and Dynamic EEG based reponse to music.
     On the side of Neuroimaging, the lab is currently developing intelligent algorithms to datamine biomarkers from clinical datasets. This is because Neurological disorders currently lack early biological markers and depend primarily on clinical and cognitive assessment for diagnosis, post disease onset.
    If this activates your neurons and you see common interest, please drop me an email for B.Tech/M.Tech projects with grade sheets (Opens every summer) in my lab. For PhD, please send a One page Research Statement and your college grade sheets. Also apply formally to IITG-BSBE PhD program.
    We work on interdisciplinary problems so it is imperative for you to understand that both of us will be learning and will be students of Science. We don't believe in BOSS culture but we gently remind each other that the work has to be done. :-) Thanks!
